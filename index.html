About Me

Hello! I'm Glen, a software engineer in Los Angeles who enjoyes building things and solving problems. I develop user-centered interfaces with modern, efficient backend systems.

Shortly after graduating from UCLA with a bachelor's degree in computer science and linguistics, I joined the Talent Path division of Genuent where I was trained in web development and consulting. Now, I work as a support engineer at Walt Disney Studios, where I utilize my technical expertise to provide unparalleled support and service to a suite of applications and their diverse users.

Additionally, I am also a full-stack engineer intern for the National Oceanic & Atmospheric Administration, where I help design and develop a new website to host informational content and training materials to suport coral reef conservation. Lastly, I am a co-teaching volunteer with the Microsoft TEALS program where I assist in teaching computer science to local high school students in efforts to help close the opportunity gap.
